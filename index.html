What 3,859 projects across the full NPS research portfolio reveal about strategic alignment.

Finding 01 — Institutional Strength

AI, Autonomy & Unmanned Systems Dominate the Portfolio

634 projects span UAS, USV, autonomous teaming, AI-enabled targeting, and human-machine collaboration. This is NPS’s deepest and most consistent research investment, with 85 currently active and 57 fleet-requested.

Finding 02 — Demand Signal

Contested Logistics: Pipeline Growing, Active Research Thin

Finding 03 — Critical Gap

Counter-UAS Is the Only True Gap in the Portfolio

Across 3,859 projects mapped against 27 strategic themes from three policy documents, dedicated counter-UAS research has just 9 projects (0.2%). Every other theme scores Moderate or Strong. This is the single structural blind spot in an otherwise well-aligned portfolio.
